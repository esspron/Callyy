## Document 3: Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

**Document Name:** `Frequently Asked Questions`

```
VOICORY FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS (FAQ)

GENERAL QUESTIONS

Q: What is Voicory?
A: Voicory is an AI voice assistant platform that creates intelligent voice agents for businesses. Our AI can handle customer calls, sales inquiries, and support requests 24/7 in multiple Indian languages.

Q: How does the AI voice agent work?
A: Our AI uses advanced speech recognition (STT), large language models (LLM), and text-to-speech (TTS) to have natural conversations. When a customer calls, the AI listens, understands intent, retrieves relevant information from your knowledge base, and responds naturally.

Q: Which languages are supported?
A: We support Hindi, English, Tamil, Telugu, Marathi, Gujarati, Bengali, Kannada, Malayalam, Punjabi, and more. Our AI can also handle code-switching (mixing languages in the same conversation).

Q: Is my data secure?
A: Yes, we take security seriously. All calls are encrypted, we're SOC 2 Type II compliant, and we offer data residency in India. We never share your data with third parties.

PRICING & BILLING

Q: How does pay-as-you-go pricing work?
A: You only pay for what you use. Voice calls are billed at ₹3 per minute, rounded to the nearest second. Add credits to your account and they never expire.

Q: Is there a minimum commitment?
A: No minimum commitment on our Starter plan. Pro and Enterprise plans have monthly subscriptions but you can cancel anytime.

Q: Do you offer refunds?
A: Yes, unused credits are fully refundable within 30 days of purchase. Usage charges are non-refundable.

Q: What payment methods do you accept?
A: We accept all major credit/debit cards, UPI, net banking, and wire transfers for enterprise customers.

TECHNICAL QUESTIONS

Q: How do I integrate Voicory with my CRM?
A: We offer native integrations with Salesforce, HubSpot, Zoho CRM, and Freshsales. For other CRMs, use our REST API or Zapier integration.

Q: What is the call latency?
A: Our AI responds in under 200 milliseconds, creating a natural conversation flow without awkward pauses.

Q: Can I use my existing phone number?
A: Yes! You can port your existing number or use our SIP trunk integration to connect your current phone system.

Q: How do I train the AI on my business?
A: Upload documents to the Knowledge Base, add text content, or let us crawl your website. The AI automatically learns from this content using RAG (Retrieval Augmented Generation).

SUPPORT

Q: What support options are available?
A: Starter: Email support (24-48 hour response)
Pro: Priority email + chat support (4-hour response)
Enterprise: Dedicated account manager + phone support

Q: Do you offer onboarding help?
A: Yes, all Pro and Enterprise customers get free onboarding assistance. Starter customers can purchase onboarding for ₹9,999.

Q: Where can I find documentation?
A: Visit docs.voicory.com for comprehensive API documentation, tutorials, and guides.
```
